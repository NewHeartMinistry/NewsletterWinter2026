A Message from Our Founders

Greetings, New Heart Family.

We are absolutely thrilled and grateful for a very meaningful New Heart in Thailand. This was our 56th New Heart, and the very first one in Thailand. God was clearly present and working amongst us, especially for the 45 candidates who were very passionate, enthusiastic and hungry for God's grace, mercy and love.

Please continue to pray for the entire New Heart Family as we continue to journey through each 4th day.

May God continue to be lifted up and honored through our New Heart Family, our churches, our families and each of us.

God bless you and keep you near to His warm, loving, gracious heart.
